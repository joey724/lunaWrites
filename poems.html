My Poems

.. 1st on guyss: If you begin to think, you’ll sink too deep, Pulled by tides where silence sleeps. But look around — such colors bloom, In every shade, life finds its room.** **We stand upon this earth so wide, Beneath the sky, with stars as guide. Wrapped in the night’s soft, endless dome, Yet ask — why leave, if this is home?** **Gaze into the heart of night, Beyond the fear, beyond the fright — There’s more to see than just the shade, There’s truth where all the light has strayed.
